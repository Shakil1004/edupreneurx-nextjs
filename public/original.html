Frequently Asked Questions

What makes EduPreneurX different from traditional universities? +

EduPreneurX (also known as CEO Institute) is the world's first revolutionary educational institute that requires students to build actual million-dollar companies while earning their degrees. Unlike traditional universities that focus only on theory, we provide real business ownership (25-50% company shares), international exposure, and guaranteed business success through our 3-cycle admission system with intimate cohorts.

How does the 3-cycle admission system work? +

Our revolutionary 3-cycle admission system operates annually: Spring (Jan-Apr), Summer (May-Aug), and Fall (Sep-Dec). Each cycle accepts only 40 students for intensive programs. This provides multiple entry points throughout the year and ensures intimate cohorts for personalized mentorship. Total annual capacity is 120 students, targeting 5,000+ students over 10 years with 100,000+ global employment creation.

What is the fee structure and why do fees become FREE after Year 2? +

Admission fee is $10,000 (one-time), followed by $1,000/month for the first 2 years ($24,000 total). From Year 2+ onwards, all fees become 100% FREE because by then, your company should be generating sufficient revenue to support your education. This revolutionary model ensures you're not accumulating debt but building wealth while studying.

What does "36% company ownership" mean? +

Unlike traditional education where you graduate with debt, EduPreneurX graduates own actual legal companies. You receive 25-50% ownership shares (varies by program) in the company you build during your studies. This means you're not just a graduate - you're a business owner with real assets, revenue streams, and equity value. The remaining shares are held by the institute and partners to ensure proper guidance and support.

What is the $1M+ company guarantee? +

EduPreneurX guarantees that you will build a company worth $1M+ by graduation. If you don't achieve this milestone, we provide FREE lifetime support, additional mentorship, and resources until you reach the $1M+ valuation. This guarantee is backed by our international business methodology, multinational partnerships, and proven track record in company building.

What international opportunities are available? +

EduPreneurX operates through World Business Groups' network spanning 65+ countries with operational presence in 22+ countries. Students receive 3+ international business trips, access to global markets, multinational company partnerships, and international college collaborations. Our offices in UK, India, Poland, and Canada provide global support and opportunities.

What makes this a CEO Institute? +

EduPreneurX is also known as CEO Institute because every graduate becomes a CEO with real company ownership. Our revolutionary approach transforms students from job seekers to job creators. With 5,000 target students over 10 years creating 100,000+ global employment opportunities, we're building the world's largest network of young CEOs and entrepreneurs.

What support is provided after graduation? +

EduPreneurX provides lifetime support including: ongoing business mentorship, access to international networks, global market expansion assistance, additional funding opportunities, alumni network connections, and continuous learning resources. We're committed to your long-term success as a global entrepreneur and business leader.
